Beautiful Beef Tacos

These absolutely perfect beef tacos will be a game changer for your families taco tuesdays.

With mouth watering pico de gallo and juicy lean ground beef its sure to make an impact.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • American cheese
  • Pico de gallo
  • Sour cream
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Cook 1lb ground beef in pan until brown.
  2. Drain grease, then melt 1 cup of American shredded cheese on top
  3. Add 2 pico de gallo
  4. Add 1/3 cup of sour cream
  5. stir then add to taco shell :)
