Perfectly Preserved Pie Recipe

Perfectly Preserved Pie

A piece of perfectly preserved pie.

Strange that of all things to survive the bombs, it would be a piece of pie.

Ingredients

  • Filling
  • 16 oz cream cheese, room temp
  • 3 eggs
  • 3/4 cups sugar
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 2 tbps cornstarch
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • Strawberry sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 275F.
  2. In a bowl combine heavy cream with your cream cheese and vanilla extract.
  3. Add sugar and mix well.
  4. Next add your eggs, then the sour cream, salt, and cornstarch
  5. Pour batter in a cheesecake pan and bake for 1 hour.
  6. Top with strawberry sauce and a plume of whipped cream.
  7. let cool in the fridge for at least 2 hours.
