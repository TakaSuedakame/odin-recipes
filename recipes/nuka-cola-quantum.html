Nuka Cola Quantum

super delicious and nutritious "mild radioactive strontium isotope,"

Nuka Cola's second version, Nuka Cola Quantum, contains an 18th fruit essence: pomegranate.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup blue Hawaiian punch
  • 1/4 cup blue raspberry sunny D
  • 3/4 cup soda water
  • 1/2 Tbps pomegranate syrup
  • 1 tsp of vanilla extract
  • Caffeine additive(optional)

Steps

  1. Stir all ingredients together, except soda water.
  2. Add soda water to mixture and stir gently.
  3. Pour into serving glass of choice.
  4. taste test, changing ingredient ratios to your liking.
  5. Enjoy the extra AP and 3rd arm :)
